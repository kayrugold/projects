Project: THE GENERAL

Classification: MATHEMATICAL ENGINE

The General is an experimental project focused on the visual representation of integer factorizations. It utilizes the SGS Poly-algo to distribute heavy prime calculations across multiple browser threads.

Technical Objectives

  • Demonstrate zero-latency UI during BigInt operations.
  • Implement a "Radar" view for composite density.
  • Export results to CSV for external cryptography analysis.
DEVELOPER NOTE
This version (v0.0.34) introduces the "Lattice View." If you experience frame drops, ensure hardware acceleration is enabled in your browser settings.
