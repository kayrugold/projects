SYSTEM: Perfected Alignment Engine

The Mission: To factor numbers that traditional brute-force methods cannot touch. Stride Hunter is built on a specific geometric theorem that views factorization as a collision of modular waves.

The Theorem

Let N = 10^e + 61
Let I = (N + 1) / 2 = 5×10^{e-1} + 31
For any odd prime p: p ∤ N ⇔ I ≢ 2⁻¹ (mod p)

This engine doesn't just "guess." It walks the modular lattice and checks for alignment at the geometric soul of the sieve. If a walker lands on the inverse of 2, a collision is detected, and the factor is revealed.
