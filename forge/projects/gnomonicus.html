Project: GNOMONICUS

Classification: ARITHMETIC VISUALIZER

Gnomonicus is a viewport into the infinite. It renders the underlying "Lattice Structure" that powers the Factor Hunter engine. By treating integers as vector coordinates rather than simple values, we can observe patterns in their Volume Sums that are invisible in standard notation.

Core Capabilities

  • Infinite Canvas: A touch-enabled viewport that supports deep zoom levels (40x to 250x scale).
  • BigInt Precision: Calculates Tape IDs and Phase IDs for integers larger than $2^{53}$.
  • Cell Inspection: Tap any node to reveal its 'Gnomon Sum'—the accumulated volume of all preceding vectors.
MATHEMATICAL NOTE
The "Volume Sum" ($\Sigma V$) is calculated by integrating the row sums from $i=0$ to $k$. This visualizer proves that the Gnomon metric holds consistency across trivial zero boundaries.
Alpha Status
This prototype is currently running on the "Glass" rendering engine. Performance may dip if you pan past $k=5000$ on mobile devices.
