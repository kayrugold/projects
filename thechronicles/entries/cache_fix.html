The Ghost in the Machine

I lost a few hours today chasing a bug that didn't exist in the code, but lived in the browser's memory. I pushed an update to the Chronicles, but my phone kept loading the version from last week.

The Culprit: Service Worker Caching.

I set up a "Cache-First" strategy to make the app load instantly offline. It worked too well. It cached index.html and refused to check the network for the new manifest file.

The Fix:

  1. Updated the CACHE_NAME in sw.js to v3.
  2. This forced the browser to treat the worker as "new" and discard the old cache.

Lesson learned: Always include a versioning strategy or a "cache-busting" mechanism when deploying static apps that rely heavily on PWA features.
