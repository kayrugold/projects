1. The Bookmark Rail

The navigation bookmarks on the right flank were previously prone to "jamming" or refusing to scroll on mobile devices.

I have rewritten the touch-event listeners to use a custom physics delta. You can now drag the bar freely up and down, regardless of how many tabs are added in the future. It no longer snaps back or fights the user's thumb.

Technomancy Update

  • Added touchmove delta tracking.
  • Disabled browser "pull-to-refresh" on the rail.
  • Implemented "Auto-Stick" positioning.
