Visualizing the Modulus

The Residue Scanner is now in Beta (v0.8). My goal was to create a "Gnomon Navigator" that highlights patterns in modular arithmetic that are usually invisible in a spreadsheet.

By mapping integers to a grid and highlighting n % k, distinct geometric artifacts appear. Specifically, when the modulus aligns with prime factors, we see clear "streets" of clear space.
