Rolling the Bones

I finally had some downtime at the truck stop to work on the collision logic for the new Dice Roller. I'm avoiding heavy libraries like Matter.js in favor of a custom, lightweight impulse engine.

The math is simple enough: check for overlap, separate the bodies, and conserve momentum. The hard part is making it "feel" like a heavy die hitting a wooden table.

Current Challenges:

  • Tunneling: At high speeds, the dice sometimes clip through the walls. I need to implement continuous collision detection (CCD).
  • Resting: The dice jitter when they should be still. I added a velocity threshold to force them to "sleep."

Next stop: Adding the texture rotation so they actually look 3D.
